⚔️ STORY OF EPIC WARS ⚔️

— THE CHRONICLE OF THE MULTIVERSE —

◈ ERA I
The Nexus Infinitum
At the center of all dimensions, there exists a force beyond comprehension — Nexus Infinitum. It takes the form of a cosmic gear, spinning eternally, radiating a natural green light that illuminates every dimension around it. It is not a place, not a creature, not a weapon. It is the very foundation of existence itself. Every galaxy, every dimension, every timeline — all of it flows from the energy of Nexus Infinitum.

Civilizations that discovered its existence built entire religions around it. All creatures spent their entire lives, trying to understand and harness its power.

Yet Nexus Infinitum cannot be controlled. Cannot be owned. It simply exists — as it always has, as it always will.
✦ ✦ ✦
◈ ERA II
The Birth of Vorrath
On the other side, there were two Entities — Lord of Light and Lord of Darkness. Neither guardians, nor rulers. They were angel-like beings who, since the very beginning of their existence, pursued only one thing — Nexus Infinitum.

But in that struggle for power, the shaken energy of Nexus Infinitum released something unplanned — a dark entity born from the collision between the ambitions of Lord of Light and Lord of Darkness.

This entity had no noble purpose. No ambition like those who indirectly created it. It had only one instinct — to destroy everything.

He is Vorrath. The Beast of Galaxy.
✦ ✦ ✦
◈ ERA III
The Great War & The Fall of Vorrath
The power of Nexus Infinitum had drawn the attention of every corner of the galaxy. Three great factions emerged — each with one identical goal — to seize the Nexus Infinitum.

The Authority of Galaxy, guardians of cosmic order. Nathasias, The Queen of Galaxy, a dark ruler hungry for power. And The Void Pirates, the native inhabitants of outer space from Planet Gloom, who would not hesitate to plunder anything of value.

A great war broke out. Galaxies became battlefields. Three factions attacked each other relentlessly, mercilessly, with no end in sight.

But in the midst of that chaos — something far greater appeared. Vorrath. The size of a black hole, destroying everything in his path. Siding with no one, he tore through the battlefield and in an instant — seized the Nexus Infinitum from the hands of all three warring factions.

The war stopped immediately. Not because of peace — but because what they had fought over was now gone.

Yet Vorrath could not harness the power of Nexus Infinitum. Time and again he tried, time and again he failed. And just as he sensed something was wrong — suddenly, from the boundless cosmic darkness, appeared a massive hand, radiating a blinding light. Its origin unknown. Its owner unknown.

That hand seized Vorrath — and hurled him far away to Planet VCrystals.

A planet filled with purple crystals emanating a deadly glow. Anyone who drew near would be weakened — and Vorrath was no exception. The giant who was once the size of a black hole slowly shrank, until he was no larger than a coconut tree.

He was chained. He was weakened. And finally — for the first time since his creation — Vorrath slept. For 5000 years.

Meanwhile, Nexus Infinitum was hurled far away — landing on a planet that almost no one believed existed. A planet that had long been considered nothing more than a myth.
✦ ✦ ✦
◈ ERA IV
The War Comes to Earth
With the disappearance of Nexus Infinitum, the three-faction war came to an end — but that did not mean peace had arrived.

The Void Pirates chose to leave — disbanding and roaming the galaxy, plundering anything of value they encountered along the way.

Yet The Authority of Galaxy and Nathasias, The Queen of Galaxy — the two of them were far from finished. Their war continued, crossing galaxy after galaxy, without end.

Until one day, in the heat of a fierce battle, The Authority of Galaxy made a cosmic leap — and landed on a small planet they had never known before.

Earth. Home of Sony.

For the first time, their feet touched the soil of Earth. And not long after — Nathasias arrived. Her manufactured planet now directly connected to Earth, turning it into a new battlefield in a cosmic conflict that never seemed to end.
✦ ✦ ✦
◈ ERA V
The Mysterious Liberator
Six years had passed since The Authority of Galaxy first set foot on Earth. Sony divided their tasks — some to rescue humanity from the threat of Nathasias' manufactured planet, others to continue fighting against her forces.

Yet in a forgotten corner of Earth — far from the battlefield, far from the noise — a mysterious human lived alone. His home was built from scraps. His surroundings were filled with discarded junk. But inside, lay technology far beyond its time — rows of PCs monitoring every corner, hacking systems that broke every limit, and a project he had been working on for 10 years.

A portal.

Not to the multiverse. Not to another dimension. But to one specific place he had studied for years — Planet VCrystals.

As he stepped through, the portal brought him exactly there. Before him, surrounded by blinding purple crystals, lay a figure that had been sleeping for 5000 years — chained, diminished, yet still radiating an undeniable aura of destruction.

Vorrath.

The mysterious human deployed his technology — two massive hands on his left and right, each gripping a great axe, and a great eye that floated faithfully behind him.

With one single strike — the chains shattered.

Vorrath slowly opened his eyes. For the first time in 5000 years, he saw the world again. And the first thing he saw — was merely a mysterious human standing calmly, gesturing for him to be free.

No words. No questions. Vorrath simply left.

And the mysterious human — smiled.
✦ ✦ ✦
◈ ERA VI
EPIC WARS Begins
Earth was no longer just a small forgotten planet in the corner of the galaxy. It had become the center of everything.

Nathasias tightened her grip. The Authority of Galaxy was pushed further back. And somewhere out there — Vorrath was free, carrying destruction that no one could predict.

And in the middle of it all — there was Sony. An ordinary human. Yet with intelligence and technology that made even cosmic entities think twice.

This war did not belong to the gods. Not to the cosmic entities. This war belonged to everyone.
